About Galiley

We are an Icelandic band of five people of which are some of the most respected, well established and greatly vexperienced musicians in the north of Iceland. We feature Freyþór Hrafn as lead singer, Gúðjón Jónsson as pianist and backing vocalist, Daniel Andri as guitarist and backing vocalist, Logi Helgason as Drummer and percussionist and Heimir Kristmundsson as electric bass player.

We have played together for many years and all have different musical background and experience complementing each other and over the years created a highly versitile band. We play everything from classic Icelandic pop from the '70s to modern pop music, and '80s disco to classic rock.

What we offer

We offer a wide range of music to suit a mixture of different events and parties. We are experienced in the Icelandic traditional countryside-party (sveitaball), weddings, corporate or industry events, school events, festivals and the traditional winter event Þórrablót. With us you find nothing but the best live music, great sound, festive lights, and always a full dance floor.

We also have excellent gear suitable for all events that we offer, everything from sound to light shows. Depending on the event, or what gear already excists at the spot we can tailor what gear to bring to your event suitible for your needs.
